Uni L. Brown's Adventures in Goodville!

Uni L. Brown is a peacemaking, progressive, young black man with kool locs, always wears yellow shades, has a shiny a gold tooth, and a luscious beard. Uni lives in GoodVille, a vibrant urban town with its pros and cons. It’s a gradually progressive community with people of color from diverse backgrounds. Even when major issues impact the community, Uni L. Brown makes sure residents work together to find solutions.
